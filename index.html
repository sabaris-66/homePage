Ash Ketchum
He is a Pokémon Trainer from Pallet Town whose goal is to become a Pokémon Master. His first Pokémon was a Pikachu that he received from Professor Oak after arriving late at his laboratory. On his journeys around the Pokémon world, he's been accompanied by a multitude of close friends. He traveled to Alola after his defeat in Kalos, there he becomes the first Champion of the Alola region's Pokémon League. In Galar, he becomes the new Monarch and World Champion of the Pokémon World Coronation Series.
