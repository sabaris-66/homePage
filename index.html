Ash Ketchum
He is a Pokémon Trainer from Pallet Town whose goal is to become a Pokémon Master. His first Pokémon was a Pikachu that he received from Professor Oak after arriving late at his laboratory. On his journeys around the Pokémon world, he's been accompanied by a multitude of close friends. He traveled to Alola after his defeat in Kalos, there he becomes the first Champion of the Alola region's Pokémon League. In Galar, he becomes the new Monarch and World Champion of the Pokémon World Coronation Series.

Ash's Top Battles

Paul Concludes The Anime's Best Storyline
Paul, Ash's ultimate rival and foil, challenges his beliefs in Pokémon training through love and respect, culminating in their epic Sinnoh League battle that sets the standard for anime story arcs.
Leon Is Ash's Most Powerful Challenge
Ash's Masters Eight battle against Leon spans four intense episodes, culminating in the ultimate Pikachu vs. Charizard showdown and solidifying it as his definitive challenge for the title of world's greatest Trainer.
Professor Kukui Celebrates Ash's Championship
Professor Kukui, as Ash's father figure in Sun & Moon, participates in a spirited six-on-six battle with Tapu Koko, resolving rivalries and showcasing their shared passion for battling.
Clemont Was Ash's Most Built Up Gym Battle
Ash and Clemont prepare intensely for their climactic Gym battle, which marks the culmination of Clemont's arc, featuring stunning action and a heartwarming resolution for Ash and his Kalos companions.
Misty Grew Alongside Ash
Misty demonstrates her deep bond with Ash in a poignant battle during Sun & Moon, showcasing respect and growth with thrilling animation and strategic depth, including Mega Evolution versus Z-Moves.
Lake Acuity Devastated Ash In The Best Way
Ash's 6-2 defeat to Paul at Lake Acuity was a pivotal moment, catalyzing significant character growth for both trainers and setting the stage for their impactful rematch in the Sinnoh League.
Brandon Was The Kanto Team's Greatest Victory
Brandon posed the ultimate challenge in Ash's Battle Frontier quest. Ash's Kanto starters secured a triumphant finale, with Pikachu's victory over a Legendary Pokémon concluding the Advanced Generation series memorably.
Gary Oak Was Ash's Original Rival
Ash's victory over Gary in the Johto League marked a turning point in their rivalry, forging friendship despite Pikachu's absence and setting a template for future rival battles with enduring significance.
Gladion Changed The Status Quo Forever
Ash's Alola League victory over Gladion marked a pivotal turning point, solidifying his Champion status and showcasing growth with Legendary and Mythical Pokémon, concluding their heartfelt rivalry.
May Proved Herself An Equal To Ash
In an unofficial Contest, Ash and May battle to a draw with their starters, Sceptile and Blaziken, showcasing May's growth to Ash's equal in their student-mentor dynamic.
Ash vs. Cynthia (Masters Eight Tournament)
Cynthia's intense battle against Ash showcased her dominance, culminating in Mega Lucario's narrow victory and inspiring her to delay retirement from battling.
Ash vs. Sawyer (Lumiose Conference)
Sawyer's admiration for Ash and their strategic clash in the Kalos League semi-final showcased him as a skilled rival, ending with Ash-Greninja's satisfying victory over Sawyer's Mega Sceptile despite the type disadvantage.
Ash vs. Iris (World Coronation Series)
Ash's battle against Iris in the World Coronation Series showcased her growth to Unova Champion, ending with Ash's Draco Meteor victory and elevation to the Ultra Class in Pokémon Journeys.
Alain vs. Ash (Lumiose Conference)
Ash's Greninja's loss to Alain's Charizard in the League Final was a dignified exit, highlighting Ash's skill and forging camaraderie with Alain, albeit missing a rematch opportunity in the Masters Eight Tournament.
Ash vs. Korrina (Shalour City Gym)
Ash and Korrina's first Gym battle with Mega Lucario marked a milestone in their rivalry and Lucario's evolution, highlighted by its visual excellence in the X&Y series, underscoring its significance in Pokémon history.
